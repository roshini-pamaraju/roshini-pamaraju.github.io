Who I Am

I'm a Generative AI & Agentic AI engineer at Accenture, Chennai, building production-grade LLM systems for enterprise clients. My focus is autonomous agent design — systems that reason, plan, retrieve, and act without human intervention in the loop.

My core specialisation is LangGraph-based multi-agent orchestration and RAG pipelines — integrating OpenAI GPT-4 and Anthropic Claude into production workflows deployed on Azure. I also own the LLMOps side: prompt engineering, model evaluation, and output monitoring.
